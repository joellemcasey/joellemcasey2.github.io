Summer and Friends

The Best Season of the Year, With the Best People

Summer is my favorite season of the year, not only because my birthday falls right in the middle of it, but because all of my friends are home from school, there's summer baseball games and concerts, the weather is great, and we are exam and homework free!

Because I live so close to a lake where all my friends have boats, I spend most of my time (when I'm not working) tubing, jet skiing, and just relaxing in the temporarily beautiful weather. I work with a lot of my friends during the summer so it doesn't make going to work as bad as it is for some people. This past summer I learned to wake board, with MANY attempts that were not successful, and few that were.

Every year I look forward to summer the most because soon enough I won't be able to do all the fun and care-free things that I do now as I'm young.
